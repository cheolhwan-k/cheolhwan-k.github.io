About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Economics at Yale University. I am on the 2026–27 job market.

My research lies at the intersection of spatial, environmental, and energy economics. I combine reduced-form empirical methods with structural modeling to study how environmental policies reshape pollution patterns and economic activity across space and over time, with applications to industrial production, power generation, and transportation.
